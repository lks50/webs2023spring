Academic Advising Center

All Pitt students are also paired with an academic advisor. According to the website for undergraduate advising, “the Academic Advising Center has a dedicated staff of full-time professional academic advisors from a broad range of backgrounds.” They suggest degree programs to help meet your career/academic goals, identify program requirements, and assist in planning an appropriate course schedule.Students should recognize the support advisors can give, and meet with them as frequently as possible.
