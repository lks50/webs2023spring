Social Wellness

Social wellness focuses on a human’s sense of connection, belonging, and well-developed support system.

Pitt Program Council

Pitt Program Council is a department in Student Life, a Division of Student Affairs, at the University. This student run club is the central programming organization on campus. The Pitt Program Council aims to connect students through a variety of social and entertainment activities. The events take place on and off campus, and all students are welcome.

Affinity Groups

The Office of Diversity & Inclusion has an abundance of affinity groups open for students to join. According to the Office of Human Resources website, “affinity groups at Pitt are a great way to connect and network with others in a welcoming, inclusive environment.” Each group is geared to different interests, cultures, and values.
