Physical Health

Physical health focuses on the state of the human body and how it operates.

Group Fitness Classes

According to the University of Pittburgh’s Student Affairs website, “becoming healthier involves adopting healthy habits. This includes incorporating physical activity into daily life.” Therefore, the University of Pittsburgh has organized a variety of high and low impact fitness classes to help students reach their goals. Intramural Sports

In addition to group fitness classes, the University has organized an intramural sports league to incorporate physical activity in a fun and social way. According to the University of Pittburgh’s Student Affairs website, “the Department of Campus Recreation offers a variety of intramural sports for undergraduate and graduate students, faculty and staff to maintain their competitive edge, remain active, or simply meet new people.” This is a great option for people who are interested in pursuing a sport, but do not have the time to commit to a club team.
